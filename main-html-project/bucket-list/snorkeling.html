Snorkeling

The ocean graces more than 70 percent of our planet, and millions of marine species call these waters home. Whether you are a novice or an experienced snorkeler, there are plenty of places to witness enchanting underwater life. All you need is a mask, snorkel, and fins to experience the beauty of coral reefs and the intriguing creatures that thrive beneath the waves. Snorkeling includes both snorkel swimming and snorkel diving. Snorkel swimming is snorkeling on the surface of the water. Snorkel diving however, includes breath-hold diving and swimming below the surface.

Best Places to Go Snorkeling

  1. Great Barrier Reef, Australia
  2. Komodo National Park, Indonesia
  3. Bay of Donsol, The Philippines
  4. Maui, Hawaii, United States
